About Us

Yam Shiping Trading, we are one of the largest suppliers of industrial and marine automation equipment. Our extensive product range includes PLCs, HMIs, Servo Drives, AC Drives, Meters, Counters, Motors, Power Supplies, Sensors, Panel Views, Displays, and much more. We source these high-quality components from trusted brands like Allen-Bradley, ABB, Siemens, Omron, Honeywell, Parker, Autronica, and Consolium. We specialize in procuring parts from surplus industrial inventories, panels of scrapped ships, and reliable discounted suppliers, ensuring that our customers receive top-tier equipment at competitive prices.

At Yam Shiping Trading, safety, quality, efficiency, and commitment are the cornerstones of our operations. Our extensive base of satisfied customers worldwide is the ultimate reward for our business. Through continuous improvements and innovations across all areas, we have carved out a niche in the Indian coastal trade while maintaining our international focus, identity, and reputation.
